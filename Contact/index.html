An Effective Bug Report

An effective bug report will contain enough information to help me find and fix the problem that you encountered.

Including as much of the following information as you can is helpful, the more the better:

Title (the e-mail subject line)
A summary of what went wrong.
Repro Steps
How to encounter the problem. Pretend that you're writing a recipe; describe how you used the software step by step to reveal the problem.
Actual Behavior
Describe what went wrong as a result of following your recipe.
Expected Behavior
Describe what should have happened a result of following your recipe.
Severity
How bad the problem is, when it happens.
Priority
How unavoidable the problem is.
Reproducibility
How reliably the repro steps reveal the problem.

An Example Bug Report

The following is a hypothetical example of an exceptionally detailed bug report. The most important parts are near the top -- give only as much detail as you are able to and comfortable with.

Title
The Job Filter Extension for LinkedIn doesn't remember new filters
Repro Steps
  1. Install the extension in Google Chrome
  2. Open the extension's side panel.
  3. (There are no filters defined.)
  4. Create an Undesirable Employer Filter for "A fictitious company name" without the quotation marks.
  5. Close the browser.
  6. Open the browser again.
  7. Open the extension's side panel.
  8. In the side panel, look at the Undesirable Employer Filters for "A fictitious company name".
Actual Behavior
There is no filter containing "A fictitious company name" under Undesirable Employers or any other category of filters.
Expected Behavior
The browser should remember the Undesirable Employer Filter that I created.
Reproducibility
100% (3 of 3 attempts in Google Chrome version 135.0.7049.42 and Microsoft Edge version 134.0.3124.93). The same thing happened on two different computers -- one Windows 10 and one Windows 11.
Severity
High - data loss (doesn't keep new data; existing data is not affected)
Priority
High - If the browser won't remember any filters after I close the browser, that means I have to add them from scratch every time I open the browser.
